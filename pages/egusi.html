Egusi Soup



What is Egusi Soup?

Egusi Soup is very delicious. It's definitely the best soup that I've ever tasted. It is so simple to make and doesn't take a lot of time.


Egusi soup is usually prepared with pounded yam, eba, amala,rice or any other swallow of your choice. I made this soup with smoked turkey but you can use any meat of your choice.


Ingredients

  • 2 cups of ground melon
  • 12 pieces of smoked turkey
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 large chopped onion
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 2 habanero peppers
  • Dried prawns
  • Palm oil
  • 2 chicken bouillon
  • 3 cup of spinach

How to Make Egusi Soup?

  1. Add ground melon to a bowl, 1 cup of water and mix to form a paste. Set aside.
  2. Turn stove on medium heat. Place a large pot on medium heat. Add palm oil and heat for 3 minutes
  3. In a blender, blend together onion, red bell peppers, and habanero peppers. Pour into pot. Bring to a boil for 5 minutes.
  4. Add the egusi paste in small portions and reduce heat. Do not stir. Just cover it with a lid. Cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Remove the lid and gently stir the soup. It will look lumpy but you can break it into the desire size.
  6. Add smoked turkey and beef stock. Stir until combined. Add dried prawns and chicken bouillon.
  7. Continue to cook for 15 minutes or more (Until your meat is tender). Check and stir to avoid burning the food. Add chopped spinach and stir for 5 minutes.
  8. Turn off the stove and allow to cool before serving.
